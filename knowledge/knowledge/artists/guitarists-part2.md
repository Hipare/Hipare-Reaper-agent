# Guitarist Sounds - Part 2

## Adam Jones (Tool)

### Signature Sound
- Dark, heavy low-tuned riffs
- Unusual time signatures
- Les Paul through Diezel/Marshall
- Minimal effects, tone from fingers

### Original Gear
- Gibson Les Paul Custom (Silverburst)
- Diezel VH4, Marshall
- Boss BF-2 Flanger, MXR Flanger

### Free VST Chain
```
1. Ignite Amps Emissary (lead channel)
   - Gain: 60-70%
   - Bass: moderate
   - Treble: slightly cut
2. NadIR with Mesa/Vintage 30 IR
3. Blue Cat's Flanger (occasional)
```

### REAPER FX Chain
```
ReaEQ:
- HPF at 80 Hz
- Cut 250 Hz by -2 dB (tighten)
- Boost 800 Hz - 1 kHz (chunk)
- Cut 3-4 kHz slightly (less harsh)
- Roll off above 8 kHz (dark)

Tuning: Drop D, Drop B, or Drop A
```

### Tips
- Let riffs breathe - space is important
- Odd time signatures: 7/8, 5/4, 9/8
- Minimal effects, maximum dynamics
- Key songs: "Schism", "Lateralus", "Forty Six & 2"

## Dimebag Darrell (Pantera)

### Signature Sound
- Razor-sharp high gain
- Scooped mids with presence
- Aggressive pick attack
- Harmonic squeals everywhere

### Original Gear
- Dean ML guitars
- Randall solid-state amps
- MXR Flanger, Dunlop Crybaby

### Free VST Chain
```
1. Ignite Emissary (LEAD channel, cranked)
   - Gain: 75-85%
   - Mids: Scooped
   - Presence: HIGH
2. NadIR with modern high-gain IR
3. ReaEQ for final scoop
```

### REAPER FX Chain
```
ReaEQ:
- HPF at 100 Hz (tight)
- SCOOP 400-600 Hz by -4 to -6 dB
- Boost 100-150 Hz (low thump)
- Boost 2-3 kHz (+3 dB) (razor cut)
- Boost 5-6 kHz for presence

NO compression - raw dynamics
```

### Tips
- Heavy pick attack, dig in
- Practice harmonic squeals (pinch harmonics)
- Tight palm muting
- Key songs: "Cowboys From Hell", "Walk", "Domination"

## Fredrik Thordendal & Mårten Hagström (Meshuggah)

### Signature Sound
- THE djent tone
- 8-string guitars, ultra-low tuning
- Tight, percussive palm mutes
- Complex polyrhythmic riffs

### Original Gear
- Ibanez 8-string guitars
- Fractal Audio, Line 6
- Very direct recording approach

### Free VST Chain - OIKEA JÄRJESTYS
```
KRIITTINEN: Efektijärjestys PITÄÄ olla tämä!

1. ReaGate (noise gate - ENSIN, leikkaa kohinan ennen vahvistusta)
   - Threshold: -40 dB to -27 dB (0.10-0.25 normalized)
   - Attack: 0-1 ms (nopea, säilyttää pick-transientit)
   - Hold: 10-50 ms (estää chatter)
   - Release: 20-50 ms
   - Hysteresis: -6 dB (0.15 normalized)

2. TSE 808 (tube screamer - tight boost, EI extra distortio!)
   - Drive: 0-15% (0.00-0.15) - MATALA! Tämä on tightness-työkalu
   - Level/Volume: 60-80% (0.60-0.80) - boostaa signaalia amppiin
   - Tone: 45-60% (0.45-0.60)

3. Ignite Emissary (amp sim - Lead-kanava)
   - LeadGain: 30-45% (0.30-0.45) - VÄHEMMÄN kuin luulet!
   - LeadBass: 30-40% - tiukka alataajuus
   - LeadLoMi: 35-45%
   - LeadHiMi: 55-65% - midien boost antaa "chunkin"
   - LeadTreb: 50-60%
   - Depth: 40-50%
   - Presence: 50-60%
   TÄRKEÄÄ: Liikaa gainia = moottorisahasoundi/fizz!

4. NadIR (cabinet IR - AMPIN JÄLKEEN, aina!)
   - Käytä SM57 + V30 cab IR:ää
   - Resonance: 40-60%
   - Ilman cab IR:ää amppi kuulostaa kamalalta!

5. ReaEQ (post-cab EQ - tonaalinen muokkaus)
   - High Pass: 80-100 Hz (poista sub-rumble)
   - Low Shelf: leikkaa -2-3 dB (poista flub)
   - Band 2 (~800Hz-1kHz): boost +2 dB (chunk/body)
   - Band 3 (~2-4kHz): boost +2-3 dB (attack/bite)
   - High Shelf: leikkaa -2-4 dB yli 8kHz (poista fizz!)

6. ReaComp (valinnainen - dynamiikan tasaus)
   - Threshold: -10 to -6 dB
   - Ratio: 2:1 - 4:1
   - Attack: nopea (1-5 ms)
   - Release: 50-100 ms

Tuning: F standard (8-string)
```

### VÄÄRÄ JÄRJESTYS (YLEINEN VIRHE):
```
ÄLÄ KOSKAAN tee näin:
- NadIR ENNEN Emissarya (cab kuuluu olla AMPIN JÄLKEEN)
- ReaGate AMPIN JÄLKEEN (gate leikkaa kohinan ENNEN vahvistusta)
- Liian korkea TSE 808 Drive (808 tuo tightnessä, EI lisä-distortiota)
- Liian korkea Emissary Gain (moottorisaha!)
```

### Tips
- Palm muting is EXTREMELY tight
- Practice with metronome constantly
- Polyrhythms against steady 4/4 feel
- Quad tracking for wall of guitars
- Key songs: "Bleed", "Rational Gaze", "Future Breed Machine"

## Stevie Ray Vaughan

### Signature Sound
- Texas blues, aggressive attack
- Tube amp cranked to breakup
- Thick strings, low action (unusual)
- Tube Screamer + clean amp

### Original Gear
- Fender Stratocaster ("Number One")
- Fender Vibroverb, Dumble
- Ibanez Tube Screamer

### Free VST Chain
```
1. TSE 808 (Tube Screamer)
   - Drive: 30-50%
   - Tone: 60%
2. LePou HyBrit or Fuse Audio F59
3. OrilRiver (spring reverb emulation)
```

### REAPER FX Chain
```
ReaEQ:
- HPF at 80 Hz
- Boost 800 Hz - 1 kHz (warmth)
- Boost 2-3 kHz (bite)
- Slight cut at 4-5 kHz (reduce harshness)
```

### Tips
- Heavy strings (.013-.058)
- Aggressive attack, dig in
- Vibrato from the wrist
- Key songs: "Pride and Joy", "Texas Flood"
