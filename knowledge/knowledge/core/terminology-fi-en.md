## Phrases for AI Understanding

**When user says → They mean:**

- "Laita kompura lauluun" → Add compressor to vocal track
- "Lisää tilaa" → Add reverb
- "Poista mutaisuus" → Cut low-mids (200-400 Hz)
- "Tee kirkkaamaksi" → Boost high frequencies
- "Vähennä ässiä" → De-ess / reduce sibilance
- "Anna ilmaa" → Boost high frequencies (air)
- "Lämmitä soundia" → Add saturation or boost low-mids
- "Tiukenna bassoa" → Tighten/clean up bass
- "Reita basso kickiin" → Sidechain bass to kick
- "Ota huone pois" → Reduce reverb/room sound
- "Tee kuivemmaksi" → Reduce reverb/effects
- "Anna enemmän paukkua" → Add punch (transients, compression)
- "Leikkaa mutaa" → Cut mud frequencies (200-400 Hz)
